02 — Backlit Glow System
The Cyan Halo Effect
The defining digital expression. From the building signage: the bird logomark is a dark silhouette with electric cyan light radiating outward from behind it. This backlit glow activates the steel foundation. Applied via box-shadow and text-shadow at varying intensities (tight, medium, wide). The glow bleeds and fades — never a sharp-edged flat color.
